About Heritage Hospice

For over two decades, Heritage Hospice has been providing compassionate, comprehensive end-of-life care to patients and families throughout Texas. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to ensuring comfort, dignity, and peace during life's most challenging moments.

What is Hospice Care?

Hospice care is a specialized type of care designed to provide comfort and support to patients and their families when a life-limiting illness no longer responds to cure-oriented treatments. Our focus shifts from curing the illness to providing comfort, pain management, and emotional support.

At Heritage Hospice, we believe that every person deserves to live their final days with dignity, surrounded by loved ones, and free from pain. Our interdisciplinary team works together to address the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of both patients and families.

Our Hospice Services

We provide comprehensive hospice care services tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient and family. Our services are designed to ensure comfort, dignity, and quality of life during this important time.

In-Home Care

Receive compassionate care in the comfort of your own home, surrounded by familiar surroundings and loved ones.

Assisted Living & Nursing Care

Specialized care for patients in assisted living facilities and nursing homes, working closely with facility staff.

Hospice Care

Comprehensive end-of-life care focusing on comfort, pain management, and emotional support for patients and families.

General In-Patient Care

Short-term inpatient care for symptom management and respite care when needed for patient comfort.

Our Health Care Professionals

Our team consists of highly trained and compassionate healthcare professionals who are dedicated to providing the highest quality of care. Each team member brings specialized expertise and a deep commitment to serving patients and families with dignity and respect.

From our medical directors and nurses to our social workers and chaplains, every member of our team plays a vital role in ensuring comprehensive care that addresses the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of our patients.

Family Resources & Support

We understand that hospice care affects the entire family. That's why we provide comprehensive support services to help families navigate this difficult journey with confidence and peace of mind.

Our family resources include counseling services, support groups, educational materials, and practical assistance with care coordination. We're here to support you every step of the way.
