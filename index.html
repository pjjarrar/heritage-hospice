Compassionate Care for Life's Final Journey

Hospice is not a place-- it's a philosophy of care. At Heritage Hospice of Texas, we embrance the journey of life with undertsanding, respect, and empathy. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for individuals and families facing a life-limiting illness, providing support, comfort, and dignity when it matters most.

What is Hospice Care?

Hospice care is a special kind of care designed to provide comfort and support to patients and their loves ones during an advanced illness, typically when curative treatment is no longer the primary focus. Instead, the emphasis shifts to symptom control, pain managment, emotional well-being, and quality of life.

Who is Eligible for Hospice Care?

Hospice care is available to anyone facing a life-limiting illness, regardless of age, diagnosis, or background. Typically, patients are eligible when their physician determines that life expectancy is six months or less if the illness runs its normal course.

Common Diagnoses Include:

  • Neurological disorders (such as ALS, Parkinson's, or Alzheimer's)
  • Heart disease
  • Lung disease
  • Kidney or liver failure
  • Cancer
  • Other serious, life-limiting conditions

Referrals to hospice can be made by physicians, hospital staff, patients, or family members. We welcome self-referrals and are happy to answer questions at any time.

Family Resources & Support

We understand that hospice care affects the entire family. That's why we provide comprehensive support services to help families navigate this difficult journey with confidence and peace of mind.

Our Support Services Include:

  • Education about the illness, prognosis, and caregiving
  • Respite care to give caregivers a needed break
  • Emotional and spiritual counseling for all loved ones
  • Assistance with advance directives, health care proxies, and practical planning

We're here to support every member of your family through this challenging time with compassion, expertise, and understanding.
