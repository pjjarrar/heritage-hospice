About Heritage Hospice

Compassionate care for over two decades

Our Story

Hospice is not a place-- it's a philosophy of care. At Heritage Hospice of Texas, we embrance the journey of life with undertsanding, respect, and empathy. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for individuals and families facing a life-limiting illness, providing support, comfort, and dignity when it matters most.

Founded in 2001, we began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional hospice care that honors each patient's unique journey. Today, we continue to uphold that mission while expanding our services and reach across the state.

Personalized Care in Your Care Community

Hospice care takes place wherever you are most comfortable-- at your care community, your home, or a family member's house. We bring our services to you. Minimizing the need for disruptive hospital visits and empowering you to make decisions about your care.

Our team respects your wishes, values and preferences. We work with you and your loved ones to develop a care plan that reflects what matters most to you, from pain management options to celebrating important milestones.

Our Promise

At Heritage Hospice of Texas, we walk beside you with respect, compassion, and unwavering support.

You are not alone.

Let us help you write the final chapter of life with comfort, meaning, and dignity.
