About Heritage Hospice

Compassionate care for over two decades

Our Story

For over two decades, Heritage Hospice has been providing compassionate, comprehensive end-of-life care to patients and families throughout Texas. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to ensuring comfort, dignity, and peace during life's most challenging moments.

Founded in 2001, we began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional hospice care that honors each patient's unique journey. Today, we continue to uphold that mission while expanding our services and reach across the state.

Our Mission & Values

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy, understanding, and genuine care for the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of our patients and families.

Dignity

We honor each person's unique journey and ensure that every patient receives care that respects their individuality and personal wishes.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards of care through continuous education, training, and commitment to best practices in hospice care.

Family-Centered

We recognize that hospice care affects the entire family and provide comprehensive support for all loved ones throughout the journey.
