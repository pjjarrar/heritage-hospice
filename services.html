Specialized Services

Beyond our core services, we offer specialized care programs designed to address specific needs and conditions.

Pediatric Hospice Care

Specialized care for children with life-limiting illnesses, providing age-appropriate support for both the child and their family.

Dementia Care

Specialized support for patients with advanced dementia and their families, focusing on comfort and quality of life.

Veterans Care

Honoring our nation's veterans with specialized care that recognizes their service and unique needs.

Bereavement Support

Ongoing support for families after the loss of a loved one, including counseling and support groups.

Your Care Team

Our interdisciplinary team works together to provide comprehensive care that addresses all aspects of your needs.

Physicians

Oversee medical care and collaborate with the patient's own doctor.

Nurses

Provide hands-on clinical care, education, and advocacy.

Social Workers

Address practical needs and offer emotional support to patients and families.

Chaplain

Support spiritual and emotional well-being, honoring all faiths and traditions.

Home Health Aides

Assist with personal care and daily living activities.

Volunteer

Offer companionship, support, and an extra pair of helping hands.

Bereavement Counselors

Guide families through grief with compassion and understanding.

Coverage & Payment

Hospice care is covered by Medicare, Medicaid, and most private insurance plans. We work with you to understand your coverage and ensure you receive the care you need.

Medicare Coverage

Medicare Part A covers all hospice services with no out-of-pocket costs for patients who meet eligibility requirements.

Medicaid Coverage

Medicaid provides comprehensive hospice coverage for eligible patients, ensuring access to quality end-of-life care.

Private Insurance

Most private insurance plans include hospice benefits. We'll work with your insurance provider to maximize your coverage.

Financial Assistance

We offer financial assistance programs for patients who may have difficulty covering the cost of care.
