Making a Referral

We work closely with physicians, hospitals, and other healthcare providers to ensure seamless transitions to hospice care. Our referral process is designed to be simple and efficient.

1

Contact Us

Call our referral line at (555) 123-4567 or complete our online referral form. We're available 24/7 to answer questions and begin the referral process.

2

Assessment

Our team will review the patient's medical records and conduct a comprehensive assessment to determine hospice eligibility and care needs.

3

Admission

If appropriate, we'll coordinate with the patient's physician to complete admission paperwork and begin hospice services within 24-48 hours.

Hospice Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for hospice care, patients must meet certain medical criteria established by Medicare and other insurance providers.

Medical Eligibility

  • Life expectancy of 6 months or less if the disease runs its normal course
  • Certification by a physician and hospice medical director
  • Patient and family choose comfort care over curative treatment

Common Diagnoses

  • Advanced cancer
  • End-stage heart disease
  • End-stage lung disease
  • Advanced dementia
  • End-stage kidney disease
  • End-stage liver disease
