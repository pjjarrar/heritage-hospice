What is Hospice Care?

Understanding compassionate end-of-life care

Understanding Hospice Care

Hospice care is a special kind of care designed to provide comfort and support to patients and their loved ones during an advanced illness, typically when curative treatment is no longer the primary focus. Instead, the emphasis shifts to symptom control, pain management, emotional well-being, and quality of life.

Hospice does not mean giving up hope--it means redefining hope. Our care team helps patients and families focus on meaningful moments, cherished traditions, and personal wishes, ensuring every day is lived to the fullest, regardless of care needs, with dignity and grace.

Who is Eligible for Hospice Care?

Hospice care is available to anyone facing a life-limiting illness, regardless of age, diagnosis, or background. Typically, patients are eligible when their physician determines that life expectancy is six months or less if the illness runs its normal course.

Common Diagnoses

  • Cancer
  • Heart disease
  • Lung disease
  • Neurological disorders (ALS, Parkinson's, Alzheimer's)
  • Kidney or liver failure
  • Other serious, life-limiting conditions

Referral Process

  • Physician referral
  • Hospital staff referral
  • Patient self-referral
  • Family member referral
  • We welcome all referrals and are happy to answer questions

Benefits of Hospice Care

Hospice care provides numerous benefits for patients and families during one of life's most challenging times. Our comprehensive approach addresses physical, emotional, and spiritual needs while providing practical support.

For Patients

  • Expert pain and symptom management
  • Comfortable care in familiar surroundings
  • Emotional and spiritual support
  • Maintenance of dignity and independence

For Families

  • Education about the illness and caregiving
  • Respite care to give caregivers needed breaks
  • Bereavement counseling and support
  • Assistance with practical planning

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you may have questions about hospice care. Here are answers to some of the most common questions we receive.

Is hospice care covered by insurance?

Yes. Most hospice services are covered by Medicare, Medicaid, and many private insruance plans. Our team will help you understand your benefits and handle the paperwork, so you can focus on what matters most.

Does hospice mean giving up?

No. Hospice is about living every moment as fully as possible. Our care supports your goals and your choices, prioritizing comfort and quality of life.

Can i keep my own doctor?

Absolutely. We work alongside your physician to ensure continuity of care.

What happens if i get better?

If your condition improves, you may choose to leave hospice care and resume regular treatment at any time.

How do i begin hospice care?

Contact us for a free, no-obligation assessment. We'll answer your questions and explain how hospice can help you and your family.
